Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets, all topped with melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, it's a hearty and comforting Italian favorite.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  4. Add minced garlic and ground beef to the skillet. Cook until the beef is browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and remaining cheeses on top.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
