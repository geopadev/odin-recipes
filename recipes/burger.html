Burger Recipe

Description

This burger recipe is a classic favorite, featuring a juicy beef patty grilled to perfection and served on a toasted bun with fresh lettuce, tomato, and your choice of toppings. It's easy to make at home and perfect for a quick lunch or a weekend cookout with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  5. If using cheese, add a slice to each patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until golden brown.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the buns and adding lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your favorite condiments.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
